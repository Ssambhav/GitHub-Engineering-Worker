# 🌟 Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and collaborative environment for everyone, regardless of experience, background, or identity.

Our goal is to build an open-source community where ideas are shared openly, feedback is constructive, and collaboration is encouraged.

---

## Expected Behavior

Members of our community are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional.
- Communicate with kindness and patience.
- Welcome different perspectives and experiences.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus discussions on improving the project.
- Help create a positive and inclusive environment.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Discrimination of any kind.
- Offensive or abusive language.
- Trolling, intimidation, or disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private or confidential information without permission.
- Any behavior that creates an unsafe or hostile environment.

---

## Collaboration Principles

We encourage contributors to:

- Prioritize technical discussions over personal opinions.
- Assume good intent.
- Respect code reviews and engineering decisions.
- Discuss disagreements professionally and respectfully.
- Work together toward improving the project.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it privately to the project maintainers.

All reports will be reviewed fairly and handled with respect and confidentiality.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct and may take appropriate action when necessary, including warning, temporary restrictions, or removal from the project.

---

## Our Goal

GitHub Engineering Worker is built on collaboration, curiosity, and continuous learning.

By participating in this project, you agree to help maintain a respectful, inclusive, and professional community where everyone can contribute with confidence.

Thank you for being part of the project. ❤️
